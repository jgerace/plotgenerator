About

Plotto, as a Method of plot suggestion for writers of creative fiction, is founded upon this law: Purpose, expressed or implied, opposing Obstacle, expressed or implied, yields Conflict.

So writes novelist William Wallace Cook in the introduction to his 1928 book, Plotto: A New Method of Plot Suggestion for Writers of Creative Fiction, in which he enumerates 1,462 plots.

This website attempts to bring those 1,462 plots to budding and experienced writers alike, displaying a random selection from Wallace's book. It also displays what Wallace called "Masterplots," generic scenarios comprised of a protagonist, an action, and a way to continue and terminate the action. Combine a Masterplot with random conflicts and a story will spring forth!
